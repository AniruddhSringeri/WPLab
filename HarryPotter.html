The Most Powerful Mages of the 20th Century

With great power comes great responsibility.
These are some of the most powerful and formidable witches and wizards of the 20th century, starting from most powerful. The list has been made based on raw power as well as skill. The list contains the most terrible dark lord in living memory(and that includes the memory of Nicholas Flamel too) who controlled the likes of Hitler and came close to subjugating the muggles; an immensely powerful dark lord who dabbled in the darkest of the dark arts; a sorcerer who is the Leader of the Light and considered to be the Greatest Wizard To Have Ever Lived; a veteran Auror and battle-hardened warrior who is responsible for the capture and the deaths of a majority of the Death Eaters; a master of the mind arts, espionage and the dark arts, who played the Dark Lord for a fool, making him think that he is a triple agent, and therefore, on his side, all the while being a Quadruple Agent; a psychopathic witch who struck terror into the hearts of everyone in Wizarding Britain by her brutality and prowess with battle-magic; and, um...how do I put this...well, um...Harry Potter.
